# Hackathon Goals & Judging Criteria

Based on discussions with the event organizers, here is what we need to aim for to maximize our chances of winning:

## 🏆 Core Requirements
- **DigitalOcean Software**: We MUST use Digital Ocean software as the foundation of our project.
- **Gradient AI Feature**: We MUST use the Gradient AI feature on DigitalOcean. This is a hard requirement to be eligible for the top 3 overall best prizes.

## 📊 Judging Focus
- **Novelty**: Judged heavily on unique and novel uses of Digital Ocean technologies.
- **Technical Complexity**: The project must demonstrate significant technical effort. It should not be obviously solvable with a single AI prompt.
- **Social Good**: The project must involve or impact social good.
- **Entertaining Demo**: The final presentation and demo must be entertaining and engaging.
- **Know the Audience**: The judges are representatives of DigitalOcean, so highlighting their platform effectively is crucial.

## 🎯 Target Prize Categories
We are aiming for the following specific prize categories:
- **Best UI/UX**
- **Best Use of Data**

*(Note: We are not eligible for the Best from New Hackers category, so it has been omitted.)*
